After a long phase of internal preparation, I would now like to work as a psychological consultant. With a successfully completed technical degree (Master of Science) and several years of professional experience, I created the right container for myself to start this new profession with the necessary care, tranquility and leisure. This allows me to take sufficient time for each of my clients.

The basis of my interest in psychology comes from personally meaningful and touching meditation experiences (Vipassana Meditation, as taught by S.N. Goenka). A regular meditation practice helps me, in contrast to the situation several years ago, to navigate my everyday live (and contact to clients) with more ease and awareness.
